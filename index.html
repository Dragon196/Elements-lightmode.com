What are elements?

The elements are the the pure substances which are made up of atoms. Number of elements is basically the number of different types of atoms that exist. The word 'atoms' means undividable, as it was thought to be undividable in the time it was predicted. There are currently 118 different types of 'atoms' or 'Elements' which are discovered till now. 92 elements are naturally occuring whereas others are artificially created in labs.

Hydrogen

So this element is first on our list. The element with atomic number 1. It is mostly recognised due to its flamablity. It is a very flamable gas which is present in the Sun. The sun crushes this gas to form a type of next element, Helium

The above structure represents an atom of hydrogen. The blue part is a proton which is positively charged and the red part is an electron which is negetively charged. The hydrogen gas, when burned in air, produces water vapour. It combine with oxygen gas when burned in air. A particular type of an atom called Isotope, is when you add more neutrons (neutral particles) to an atom. This may create an unstable atom. An such isotope of Hydrogen, called Deuterium, is an example. Its combination with Oxygen make Heavy Water. Another isotope of Hydrogen, is Tritium. It is very rare and is sometimes used in process of nuclear energy generation.
